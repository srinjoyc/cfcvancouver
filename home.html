What is Building Bridges?

This event will be the first of its kind, offering a cross-cultural management case study competition to 75 hand-picked Canadian university students. The competition will offer mentorship to these students throughout the day long competition, followed by their presentations to 160 delegates and 4 judges. Delegates will include business leaders, entrepreneurs, government officials, and thought leaders from throughout Canada.

The event will focus on the topic of scale and look to the Asian market as an example of serving 4.5 billion people. Attending university students will get a chance to work on current problems faced by companies in Asia, and the winning teams will have their proposals sent directly to the companies in question. Students may very well get a chance to see their solutions being implemented in Asia!
